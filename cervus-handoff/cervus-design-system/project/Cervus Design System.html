02 · Principles

Four postures.

Editorial restraint over enterprise blue. Density without crowding. Weight earned through typography, never through saturation.

i.
Velvet by default
Dark surfaces, hairline borders, almost no shadow. Depth comes from layering ink, not from drop shadows.
ii.
Serif for weight
Instrument Serif handles the moments that matter — covers, hero numbers, the three priorities. Geist runs the rest.
iii.
One accent, sparingly
Stag teal carries focus, live state, and a single hero CTA. Never decorative, never as a fill.
iv.
Risk is editorial
The four AI Act levels are pigments, not warnings. Severity is carried by the article reference, not the swatch.
