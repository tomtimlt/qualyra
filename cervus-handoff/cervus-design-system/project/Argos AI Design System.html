02 · Principles

Four postures.

Institutional restraint over enterprise blue. Density without crowding. Weight earned through hierarchy, never through saturation.

01
Quiet by default
Slate surfaces, hairline borders, measured contrast. Depth comes from layering ink — not from drop shadows or gradients.
02
One family
Geist runs the entire system. Display is heavy + tight; body is regular. No editorial serif, no decorative italic.
03
One accent
Argos navy carries focus, the live state, and a single hero CTA. Never decorative. Never as a fill on body copy.
04
Risk is data
The four AI Act levels are muted, institutional pigments. Severity travels with the article reference, not the swatch.
